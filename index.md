# Planova — Legal Hub

**Planova** is an offline-first personal finance, habits, and planning mobile application developed by Mykyta Zhorov.

This page is the official legal hub for the Planova app. All legal documents governing your use of the application are published here. By using Planova, you agree to the documents listed below.

---

## What Are These Documents?

These documents are legally binding agreements between you (the user) and the developer of Planova. They explain:

- **What data we collect** and why
- **How your data is stored, used, and protected**
- **Your rights** as a user (including deletion, correction, and access)
- **The rules of using** the Planova application
- **How to delete** your account and all associated data

These documents apply to all versions of the Planova mobile application available on Google Play.
