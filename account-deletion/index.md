# Account Deletion / Data Deletion Instructions (Planova)

**Effective date:** 2026-03-11

This page explains how to delete your Planova account and associated data.

## Delete your account from within the app

1. Open the Planova app
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Tap **Delete account**
4. Confirm deletion

## What is deleted

When you delete your account, Planova is designed to remove:

- Your cloud data stored under your account in Firebase services (for example, Firestore documents)
- Your uploaded files in Firebase Storage (for example, avatar photo, if any)
- Your authentication account (Firebase Authentication)
- Local app data stored on your device (as part of the deletion flow)
